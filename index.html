Race Info

          

            The Great Mill Race is a community road race set in a beautiful park setting,             featuring iconic historic buildings and lakes along the way. Choose the 5K for a             classic run or the 1.5 Mile for a shorter option.           

          

            We’ll publish the official course map, schedule, and parking details here as we finalize logistics.
